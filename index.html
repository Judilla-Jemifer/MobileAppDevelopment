Sample case:

We are to input an amount we want to save one time, the interest rate, as well as the number of years we want to save. We also input our expected or desired amount after saving for those number of years. The output will be the savings we have for each year from the first to the last, the total interest we get, and a message saying whether the amount is more than our expected amount or less than that. This is applicable in situations like investing one time with a guaranteed or expected interest rate per year that is compounding monthly.
